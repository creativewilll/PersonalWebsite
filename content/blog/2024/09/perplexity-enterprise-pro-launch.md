## What Is Perplexity Enterprise Pro?

**Perplexity Enterprise Pro is the business-tier version of Perplexity's AI search platform**, designed for organizations that need enterprise-grade security, team management, and internal knowledge capabilities alongside Perplexity's real-time search features.

Unlike the consumer Pro tier, Enterprise Pro adds:
- SOC 2 Type II compliance for security-conscious organizations
- Single Sign-On (SSO) with corporate identity providers
- SCIM support for automated user provisioning
- Internal Knowledge Search across company documents
- Team collaboration features with Spaces and shared collections
- Data privacy guarantees with 7-day automatic deletion
- Administrative controls and usage analytics

The core search experience remains unchanged—users still get Perplexity's signature real-time answers with cited sources, access to multiple LLM models, and the clean, citation-first interface that differentiates it from chat-based AI tools.

But Enterprise Pro wraps that experience in the security and management layer that IT departments require before approving any new AI tool for company-wide deployment.

## Security and Data Privacy Features

**The security architecture is where Enterprise Pro justifies its price tag.** Perplexity has built the product around three core guarantees that address the primary concerns IT departments have about AI tools.

### SOC 2 Type II Certification

Enterprise Pro carries SOC 2 Type II certification—the gold standard for enterprise SaaS security. This isn't a self-assessment; it's an independent audit validating that Perplexity's security controls meet strict criteria for:

- **Security**: Protection against unauthorized access
- **Availability**: System uptime and reliability
- **Processing integrity**: Data processing accuracy and completeness
- **Confidentiality**: Protection of sensitive information
- **Privacy**: Proper handling of personal information

For organizations in regulated industries or with strict vendor requirements, this certification removes a major procurement barrier.

### Data Privacy Guarantees

Perplexity makes three explicit promises that differentiate it from consumer AI products:

1. **Data never trains Perplexity's models** — Your queries, uploads, and internal content stay strictly yours. They're not used to improve Perplexity's LLMs or shared with model providers.

2. **7-day automatic deletion** — Queries and uploaded files are automatically deleted after 7 days, reducing the window of exposure for sensitive information.

3. **No data retention for model inference** — When Perplexity routes queries to underlying models (OpenAI GPT-4, Claude, etc.), your data isn't retained by those providers either.

### Threat Management and Monitoring

Enterprise Pro includes real-time alerts for suspicious behavior and risky activities. Administrators receive notifications when:

- Unusual query patterns emerge
- Sensitive keywords are searched
- Bulk data extraction attempts occur
- Login anomalies are detected

This monitoring layer gives security teams visibility into how the AI search tool is being used across the organization.

## User Management and Access Control

**Enterprise Pro transforms Perplexity from a personal tool into a managed team platform.** The access control features are designed for organizations with hundreds or thousands of users.

### Single Sign-On (SSO)

Enterprise Pro integrates with standard corporate identity providers through SAML 2.0:

- **Okta**
- **Microsoft Azure AD**
- **Google Workspace**
- **OneLogin**
- **Custom SAML providers**

This means users authenticate through your existing identity infrastructure—no separate Perplexity passwords, no credential sprawl, and centralized offboarding when employees leave.

### SCIM Provisioning

System for Cross-domain Identity Management (SCIM) enables automated user provisioning:

- **Automatic account creation** when employees are added to the right groups
- **Role assignment** based on directory attributes
- **Automatic deprovisioning** when access is revoked
- **Group-based access controls** for different teams or departments

For IT teams managing hundreds of SaaS applications, SCIM support eliminates the manual work of adding and removing users.

### Administrative Controls

Enterprise administrators can:

- View usage analytics and search trends
- Manage team Spaces and collections
- Set content filtering and domain restrictions
- Configure data retention policies
- Export audit logs for compliance

These controls give organizations governance over how AI search is used, without blocking the productivity gains that make Perplexity valuable.

## Internal Knowledge Search Capabilities

**The most transformative Enterprise Pro feature is Internal Knowledge Search**—the ability for teams to search their own documents, wikis, and knowledge bases using Perplexity's AI search interface.

### How It Works

Internal Knowledge Search connects to your organization's existing content repositories:

- **Notion workspaces**
- **Google Drive folders**
- **SharePoint sites**
- **Confluence spaces**
- **Slack channels**
- **Custom document uploads**

Users can ask questions like "What's our Q3 marketing strategy?" or "How do we handle enterprise security reviews?" and receive synthesized answers drawn from internal documents, with citations pointing back to the source files.

### Spaces for Team Organization

Enterprise Pro introduces **Spaces**—dedicated workspaces for teams, projects, or functions:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **File Repository** | Upload up to 100 files per Space (PDFs, docs, spreadsheets) |
| **Domain Filters** | Customize which domains are included in web searches |
| **Shared Threads** | Team members can view and continue each other's research sessions |
| **Collections** | Save and organize important answers for future reference |
| **Permissions** | Control who can access, edit, or administer each Space |

A sales team might have a Space with competitive battlecards and pricing documents. An engineering team might maintain a Space with architecture decisions and API documentation. Each Space operates as a siloed knowledge base with its own upload limits and access controls.

### Connector Architecture

Perplexity provides "easy-to-use connectors" to external data sources. While the full connector library isn't publicly documented yet, the emphasis is on:

- **No-code setup** — Configure connections through the Perplexity interface
- **Real-time syncing** — Content updates flow through to search results
- **Permission respect** — Connectors honor existing access controls from the source system
- **Selective indexing** — Choose which folders, channels, or spaces to include

This connector approach lets organizations surface institutional knowledge without migrating everything to a new platform.

## File Upload and Research Features

**Enterprise Pro removes the upload limits that constrain consumer-tier users**, enabling serious research workflows that involve large document collections.

### Unlimited File Uploads

Unlike the standard Pro tier's upload quotas, Enterprise Pro offers:

- **Unlimited file uploads** across the organization
- **Up to 100 files per Space** for focused research projects
- **Support for PDFs, Word documents, text files, and spreadsheets**
- **Automatic text extraction and indexing**

This capacity supports use cases like:
- Analyzing earnings call transcripts across quarters
- Comparing RFP responses from multiple vendors
- Researching across hundreds of academic papers
- Building a searchable archive of legal documents

### Search-Assisted Queries

File uploads integrate with Perplexity's real-time search capabilities. Users can:

1. Upload a collection of documents to a Space
2. Ask questions that synthesize information from uploaded files **and** live web sources
3. Receive answers that cite both internal documents and current web sources
4. Compare internal knowledge against external benchmarks or news

This hybrid approach is unique—most AI tools handle either internal documents OR web search. Perplexity combines both in a single query flow.

### Research Workflows

The combination of Spaces, file uploads, and shared threads enables structured research workflows:

1. **Create a Space** for the research project
2. **Upload source materials** (competitor filings, industry reports, internal data)
3. **Execute queries** to synthesize findings across sources
4. **Share threads** with stakeholders for review
5. **Export or save** key findings to Collections
6. **Archive or delete** the Space when the project concludes

For research-heavy teams—investment analysis, competitive intelligence, academic research—this workflow replaces hours of manual document review with AI-assisted synthesis.

Enterprise Pro requires annual billing at $400 per user per year. There's no monthly option, which reflects the product's positioning for committed organizational deployment rather than individual experimentation.

These gaps suggest room for future tier differentiation or add-on features.

## Integration and Deployment Considerations

**Getting Enterprise Pro deployed requires coordination between IT, security, and end users.** Here's what organizations need to plan for.

### Technical Requirements

**Identity Provider Integration:**
- SAML 2.0-compatible identity provider (Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, etc.)
- Admin access to configure SSO application
- Ability to assign users to the Perplexity app through existing groups

**Connector Setup (Optional):**
- Admin access to connected systems (Notion, Google Drive, etc.)
- Decision on which content to index vs. exclude
- Potential security review of connector permissions

**Network Considerations:**
- Perplexity requires standard HTTPS access (ports 443)
- No VPN or special network configuration typically required
- No on-premise software installation

### Rollout Strategy

**Phased Deployment:**
1. **Pilot phase** — Deploy to a single team (20-50 users) for 2-4 weeks
2. **Feedback collection** — Gather input on use cases, gaps, and training needs
3. **Policy documentation** — Establish guidelines for appropriate use, sensitive data handling
4. **Broader rollout** — Expand to additional teams based on pilot learnings
5. **Full deployment** — Organization-wide access with governance in place

**Training Requirements:**
- Perplexity's interface is intuitive, but training on Enterprise-specific features (Spaces, connectors, citations) accelerates adoption
- Plan for 30-60 minutes of initial training per user
- Document internal best practices for search techniques and source verification

### Governance and Policy

Organizations should establish:

- **Acceptable use policy** — What can and cannot be searched/uploaded
- **Data classification guidelines** — Which sensitivity levels are appropriate for Perplexity
- **Citation standards** — When and how to verify AI-sourced information before using it
- **Offboarding procedures** — Ensuring data is removed when employees leave

## What's Missing and What's Next

**Enterprise Pro is a strong foundation, but gaps remain** that suggest where the product is heading.

### Current Limitations

**No API Access:**
Unlike ChatGPT Enterprise, Perplexity Enterprise Pro doesn't offer API access at launch. This limits integration with:
- Custom internal tools
- Workflow automation platforms (n8n, Zapier, Make)
- Existing research and intelligence platforms
- CRM and sales intelligence systems

**Limited Connector Ecosystem:**
While connectors exist for major platforms (Notion, Google Drive), the ecosystem is narrower than established enterprise search tools like Glean or Coveo.

**No Custom Model Fine-Tuning:**
Organizations can't train Perplexity on company-specific vocabulary, acronyms, or document structures. This limits accuracy for highly specialized industries.

**No On-Premise Option:**
Cloud-only deployment may be a blocker for organizations with strict data sovereignty requirements or air-gapped environments.

### Likely Roadmap Directions

Based on the launch positioning and competitive landscape, expect Perplexity to add:

1. **API tier** — Programmatic access for integration workflows
2. **Expanded connectors** — More enterprise systems (Salesforce, Jira, ServiceNow)
3. **Workflow automation** — Built-in integrations or partnerships with automation platforms
4. **Advanced analytics** — Deeper insights on search patterns and knowledge gaps
5. **Custom model options** — Organization-specific tuning or retrieval augmentation
6. **Mobile enhancements** — Better iOS/Android apps for field teams
