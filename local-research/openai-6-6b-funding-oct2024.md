# OpenAI's $6.6 Billion Funding Round (October 2024)

## Overview
OpenAI successfully closed the **largest venture capital round of all time**, raising **$6.6 billion** and achieving a **$157 billion post-money valuation**. This funding round solidified OpenAI's position as one of the world's most valuable private companies and the leader in generative AI.

## Lead Investor & Key Participants

**Thrive Capital** led the round with approximately **$1.25-$1.3 billion** invested, including an exclusive option to invest up to **$1 billion more at the same valuation through 2025**.

Other major investors included:
- **Microsoft**: ~$1 billion (OpenAI's key cloud partner)
- **SoftBank**: $500 million
- **NVIDIA**: $100 million
- **Khosla Ventures**: Participated (early OpenAI backer)
- **Altimeter Capital**, **Fidelity**, **Tiger Global**, and **MGX**: Also participated

**Notable:** Apple was reportedly in discussions but did not participate.

## Use of Funds
The capital will be deployed toward:
- Expanding AI research capabilities and frontier AI leadership
- Scaling compute infrastructure for ChatGPT and GPT-4
- Developing next-generation AI models
- Advancing progress toward artificial general intelligence (AGI)

## Key Terms & Conditions

**Restructuring Requirement**: Investors were granted the option to reclaim their investments if OpenAI fails to transition from its nonprofit governance model to a for-profit structure within **two years**.

**Investor Restrictions**: OpenAI reportedly requested that investors avoid backing rival AI startups, including **Anthropic** and **xAI**.

## Impact
- **Total funding raised to date**: $17.9 billion
- This round represents a historic milestone as the largest VC funding round ever completed
- OpenAI's $157 billion valuation places it in the same league as publicly traded companies like Uber and AT&T
