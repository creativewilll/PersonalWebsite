### Adobe Firefly Video Model Public Beta Launch and Key Details

#### Launch Timeline
- **Public Beta Launch**: October 2024 (initial limited public beta announced at Adobe MAX on October 14, 2024, expanding Adobe's Firefly family). Earlier previews were in September 2024 (e.g., September 11 announcement), with a waitlist for beta access. By early 2025 updates (e.g., CG Channel Feb 2025), it was fully in public beta.
- Not a September launch for public beta—beta rolled out post-preview in October.

#### Key Features (Beta Capabilities)
Powered by the Firefly Video Model, integrated into Firefly web app, Premiere Pro (Generative Extend beta), and planned for After Effects/Frame.io. Focuses on commercial workflows for video editors.

| Feature | Description | Specs (Beta Limits) |
|---------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Text to Video** | Generate 1080p video clips from text prompts. Controls: camera angle, motion, zoom, shot size (e.g., extreme close-up to long shot), aspect ratios (16:9 or 9:16). Reference images for B-roll to fill timeline gaps. | 5 seconds max length, 1080p. 4K planned soon. |
| **Image to Video** | Animate still images/illustrations into video clips. Enhanced photorealism, text rendering, new resolutions/aspect ratios. | 1080p, 5s clips. |
| **Generative Extend** | AI extends clips with perfect timing for edits in Premiere Pro. | Beta in Premiere Pro. |
| **Other Enhancements** | 4x faster image gen (related models); ProRes exports 3x faster; hardware acceleration; expanded camera support (Canon, Sony, ARRI, RED). 3D scene references for images. | Integrated across Creative Cloud, Express, Photoshop, Illustrator. |

- **Access**: Firefly web app (firefly.adobe.com) or Premiere Pro. Sign up for beta notifications if needed.

#### Commercial Safety and Training
- **Commercially Safe**: First publicly available video model explicitly "designed to be safe for commercial use." Trained on Adobe Stock (licensed, public domain, CC0 content)—**no user-generated or third-party artist data**. Content Credentials baked in for provenance/tracking. Expands safe Firefly family (Image, Vector, Design Models).

#### Pricing and Plans
- Included in **select Adobe Creative Cloud plans** (e.g., All Apps).
- Standalone: **Firefly Standard** or **Pro** subscriptions for more AI credits/generations.
- Details: Visit [Adobe Firefly Plans page](https://www.adobe.com/products/firefly/plans.html). Free tier limited; Pro for heavy use (exact credits/pricing vary by region—e.g., ~$4.99–$29.99/mo historically for Firefly, check site for 2026 updates).
