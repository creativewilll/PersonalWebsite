### Amazon-Anthropic Investment Announcements (November 2024)

- **Date**: November 22, 2024
- **Sources**:

- **Partnership Scope**:
  - Anthropic to use AWS Trainium and Inferentia chips for building, training, and deploying future models.
  - Long-term commitment for AWS customers to access Anthropic's foundation models via **Amazon Bedrock**.
  - Focus on generative AI workloads, safety research, and scalable performance.
