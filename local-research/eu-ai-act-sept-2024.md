### EU AI Act Implementation Timeline: Focus on Prohibited AI Practices (as of September 2024)

The EU AI Act entered into force on **1 August 2024** (20 days after publication in the Official Journal on 12 July 2024). It features a **staggered implementation timeline**, with prohibitions on unacceptable-risk AI systems (Chapter V, Article 5) applying **early** compared to other obligations.

#### Key Status in September 2024
- **No major requirements enforceable yet**: As of September 2024, the Act is in force but **no compliance obligations apply**. The first prohibitions and related rules begin **2 February 2025** (6 months after entry into force).
- Prohibited practices (e.g., manipulative subliminal techniques, social scoring by governments, real-time remote biometric identification in public spaces for law enforcement except narrow exceptions, emotion recognition in workplaces/education) are **not yet enforceable**.
