#### Key Takeaways for Operations Teams
- **Fastest ROI Drivers**: Data entry/reporting (20-50% time cuts), onboarding/invoicing (30-80% savings). Track **time saved → labor cost → revenue uplift** (e.g., faster fulfillment).
- **Calculation Framework**: (Hours Saved × Hourly Rate × 52 Weeks) - Automation Cost = Net Savings. Add intangibles like error reduction (e.g., 40% order entry drop).
- **Average Payback**: 3-6 months; scales with team size (e.g., $12K-$155K/month savings).
- **Pro Tips**: Baseline manual hours first, integrate with ERP/CRM, measure beyond hours (e.g., 20% speed gains per Forrester).
