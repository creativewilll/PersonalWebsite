## Announcement Overview
Adobe officially previewed its Firefly Video Model on **September 11, 2024**, introducing groundbreaking generative AI capabilities for video creation and editing.

## Key Features Announced

### Text to Video
- Generate video directly from text prompts
- Access to multiple camera controls (angle, motion, zoom)
- Ability to reference images for B-Roll generation
- Seamlessly fill gaps in video timelines

### Image to Video
- Transform still shots or illustrations into live-action clips
- Bring static images to life with dynamic video

### Generative Extend (Premiere Pro)
- Extend video clips by up to 2 seconds
- Integrated directly into Premiere Pro for professional workflows
