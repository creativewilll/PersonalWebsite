# Perplexity Enterprise Pro: Launch, Features, Pricing & Security

Based on the search results, here's comprehensive information about Perplexity Enterprise Pro:

## Launch Timeline
Perplexity Enterprise Pro was rolled out to select companies over several months, with notable early adopters including Stripe, Zoom, Bridgewater, Snowflake, the Cleveland Cavaliers, Universal McCann, Thrive Global, Databricks, Paytm, ElevenLabs, HP, Vercel, and Replit.

## Key Features

**Data Privacy & Security:**
- Data stays with the organization—never used to train Perplexity's LLMs
- SOC II Type II certification (gold standard in security)
- Real-time alerts for suspicious behavior and risky activities
- 7-day automatic deletion of queries and uploaded files
- Enhanced security protocols for threat management and data loss prevention

**User & Access Management:**
- Single Sign-On (SSO) integration with corporate identity providers
- SCIM support for streamlined user provisioning
- Easy user addition/removal for team management
- Internal Knowledge Search capabilities

**File & Research Capabilities:**
- Unlimited file uploads and search-assisted queries
- Upload up to 100 files per repository/Space
- Customizable domain inclusion in searches
- Easy-to-use connectors to external data sources

## Pricing

- **Enterprise Pro**: $40/user/month ($400/year)
- **Enterprise Max** (announced later): $325/user/month ($3,250/year)
- **Government Edition**: $0.25 per agency for first 15 months

## Business Tier Focus

Enterprise Pro targets organizations requiring enterprise-grade security while maintaining productivity and leveraging advanced AI capabilities for complex research and tasks.
